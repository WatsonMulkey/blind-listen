    Welcome to Blind Listen

    What is this?

    A free tool for unbiased mix comparison. Upload your mixes, listen with hidden identities, then reveal which is which. No account required — everything runs in your browser.

    How it works

    1. Drop 2–5 audio files (WAV, MP3, FLAC, etc.)
    2. Click Start Listening — files are randomly assigned to X, Y, Z buttons
    3. Switch between mixes, take notes, loop sections
    4. Click Reveal to see which file was which
    5. Export your results as text or PDF
